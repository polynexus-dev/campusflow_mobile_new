Anti-Proxy Blueprint

Privacy-First Security,
Engineered for Trust

Every layer of the CampusFlow security stack is designed not just to prevent fraud, but to do so in a way that respects student privacy, satisfies data protection regulations, and provides irrefutable audit evidence.

  • One-Device Binding Student accounts are cryptographically locked to a unique physical device fingerprint. Transferring attendance to a friend's phone is architecturally impossible.
  • Cryptographic TOTP Tokens QR codes are signed with HMAC-SHA256 using Django's secret key salted with a 15-second time bucket. Expired tokens are mathematically invalid — no server-side check needed.
  • GDPR-Compliant Facial Hashes CampusFlow never stores raw facial imagery. Face landmark meshes are computed on-device, transmitted as floating-point vectors, matched server-side, and the source image is discarded within 30 seconds.

Workflow

From Lecture Start to
Verified Attendance in 90 Seconds

A seamless flow designed to be invisible to honest students, and impenetrable to those trying to game the system.

Step 01

Lecturer Checks In

The lecturer opens CampusFlow, taps "Start Session." Their live GPS anchors the classroom boundary in real-time.

Step 02

Student Liveness Check

Student opens the app. A random motion challenge is issued — blink twice, turn left — completed in under 10 seconds.

Step 03

Geofence Verified

Student's GPS is cross-validated against the lecturer's classroom anchor. Spoofers are detected and blocked server-side.

Step 04

Attendance Confirmed

All checks pass in milliseconds. Attendance is marked, timestamped, and logged immutably — zero manual effort.
