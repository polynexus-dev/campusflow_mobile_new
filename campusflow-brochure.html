02 / Campus Vulnerabilities

Securing Classroom Integrity

Traditional rolls and simple digital cards are failing. When check-ins are easily spoofed, the value of academic attendance records drops to zero.

The Loophole: Peer Proxy Check-ins

Students check in for absent friends by sharing static QR code screenshots, passing physical cards, or forwarding static geolocation coordinates via email.

The Hack: Geofence Geolocation Spoofing

Basic static coordinates checkers are easily bypassed using virtual GPS apps. Absent students mark themselves present straight from their hostels.

The Drain: Administrative Roll Call Overhead

Instructors spend the first 15 minutes of every class calling out roles or adjusting paper spreadsheets, losing weeks of teachable hours every semester.

The Solution: CampusFlow Attendance

CampusFlow establishes a high-integrity, automated verification loop. By marrying device motherboard signatures, liveness checks, and rotating proximity algorithms, attendance validation becomes secure, instant, and completely hands-off for professors.

03 / Features Grid

Core Technology Pillars

Four integrated security layers designed to streamline check-ins, verify student locations, and simplify classroom management.

Step 1

Pose-Validated Enrollment

Students capture multi-angle (front, left, right) facial coordinate profiles. Coordinates are verified by backend models to check lighting and profiles, locking them to a hardware profile signature.

Step 2

Randomized Liveness Check

To verify check-in, students perform random challenges (blinking, nodding, or profile turns). Prevents spoofing via printed face pictures, video displays, or masks.

Pillar 3

Dynamic Geofence Anchor

No static coordinates database required. Lecturers check in live to drop their coordinates, automatically anchoring a dynamic presence geofence around the classroom location.

Pillar 4

Rotating QR Fallback

Solves classroom GPS drift and signal outages. Displays an encrypted rotating QR code that refreshes every 15 seconds. Scanning confirms local classroom presence instantly.
